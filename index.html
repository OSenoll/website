About ALFAS

With over 15 years of experience in the aluminum industry, ALFAS has established itself as a leading provider of innovative facade solutions, composite panels, and architectural systems worldwide.

We have undertaken numerous projects across continents, from Africa to Europe, Asia to the Americas. Our commitment to precision, quality, and innovation has made us the trusted partner for architects, contractors, and developers.

Our expertise includes aluminum glass facade systems, composite facade cladding, pergola systems, and comprehensive project management services.
